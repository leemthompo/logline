Our Services

1. Text-to-Text

You provide us with preparatory material in text form. We then draft a pristine English document for your target audience. We can use your existing presentations, rough notes and even French-language text as the baseline for our documentation.

2. Audio-to-Text

Send us your recordings and we prepare summaries, reports/minutes or tailored public-facing copy. These can be recordings of internal meetings, EWC sessions, analysts calls or conference events.

3. Bespoke Writing Services

This is our most tailored option. We work hand-in-hand to prepare unique, ad-hoc projects or to develop an ongoing collaboration for your English language copy. 4. On-Site Missions

You need a writer to come on site and attend a board meeting, EWC session or other corporate engagement. We record the session, our writer takes notes and we produce the target document of your choice. These can be as detailed as a full transcript or as brief as a compact summary.
