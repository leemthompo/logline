Simple, transparent pricing

Business Documentation

Our pricing follows a simple, linear scale. There are 3 variables which determine the final cost of a given project:

  1. The detail of the document (1 page = 400 words)
  2. If you need the writer to attend a live session
  3. If you need a rapid turnaround (< 5 working days)

In other words, if you send us a recording of the event and you don't need a rapid turnaround, then you will pay exactly what you see outlined in the table.
