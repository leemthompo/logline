How does your pricing work?

We have a baseline of pricing for our on-site missions and standard documents, on a per-hour basis + variable fees (travel, overnight expenses etc.) For our more bespoke services, we will send you a quick quote once you outline what you're looking for. As there are a large number of variables involved in any given writing project, it's hard to provide concrete pricing upfront. Nevertheless, we do our due diligence upfront, to avoid additional costs throughout the project.

How does Quick Quote work?

Send us a request outlining exactly what kind of documentation you require:

  1. Technical writing
  2. Meeting Transcript/Summary
  3. Bespoke Services
  4. Longform Project (whitepaper, research piece etc.)

Don't forget to mention whether you need your writer to come on-site.

How do we share files?

We find that it's easiest to simply share files with whatever system your team habitually uses.

Is logline an agency?

No. This is why we can attract customers that are priced out by agency markups.

How do you take payments?

For French corporate customers, traditional invoicing is possible. We are in the process of setting up a Stripe payment checkout for simple online payments.

Do you have a quality guarantee?

To ensure that you get what you need, we'll send you a preliminary draft (usually 2-3 pages), to iron out any issues of style or methodology. We will go the extra mile to ensure you are 100% satisfied with the final work.

Can I get a sample document?

Confidentiality is a critical aspect of our work, so we can't share any proprietary documents, but we are happy to prepare a one-pager to give you visibility on what your completed documentation will look like.
